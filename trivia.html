Trivia Basics

Trivia--also known as pub trivia, pub quiz, Quizzo, and other similar names--is a team-based competitive game generally played in a bar. The trivia host (quiz master) announces trivia questions in a series of rounds, while teams keep track of their answers on paper that they submit at the end of each round. In most trivia versions, each correct version wins the team a single point, and points are tallied and tracked until the end of the game. The game ends when all rounds have been completed and the winner--the team with the most points--is declared.
