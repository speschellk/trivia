    About

    We get it. You're a bar trivia (pub quiz, Quizzo, Trivial Pursuit, etc.) ninja. Or maybe you're a newbie prepping for your very first trivia experience. Either way, Trivial is designed to help you quickly improve your chances of victory--just in time for that next game. We pull from the freshest and most relevant YouTube content to present you with factoids from some of the most common trivia categories. Knowing more right before your game, and you just might be able to impress your teammates with more correct answers.

    How do we choose YouTube videos?

    We use YouTube's API to access videos on the "Most Popular" playlists that are tagged with the categories you click.
